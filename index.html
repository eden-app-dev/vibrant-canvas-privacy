This Privacy Policy describes how Vibrant Canvas ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the application on Android devices. Please read it carefully. By downloading or using the app you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Analytics data — Google Firebase

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. Firebase automatically collects the following information when you use the app:

  • A randomly generated anonymous user ID (not linked to your identity)
  • Device model, manufacturer, and screen resolution
  • Android OS version and language setting
  • App version and build number
  • How you installed the app (e.g., Google Play Store)
  • Country and region (derived from IP address; the IP itself is not stored)
  • First open date and time of each session

In addition, we collect the following gameplay events to understand and improve the player experience:

  • Which levels you start, complete, or exit early, and how long each takes
  • Ratings you assign to completed levels (1–5 stars)
  • How long you spend on the level selection screen before choosing a level
  • Font size and display settings you configure in Settings
  • Total session duration and number of sessions
  • Achievements unlocked
  • Whether and when you interact with optional in-app purchase prompts
  • When an advertisement is shown or dismissed
We never collect your name, email address, phone number, location beyond country level, or any information that identifies you personally. All analytics are tied to an anonymous ID that cannot be traced back to you.

Firebase analytics data is transmitted to and stored on Google servers, which may be located outside your country, including in the United States. See Section 6 (International Transfers) for details.

Firebase is operated by Google LLC under their Privacy Policy.

1.2 Advertising data — Google AdMob

The free version of the app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect:

  • Your device's Android Advertising ID (AAID) — a resettable, non-personal identifier
  • Approximate location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • Device type, OS version, and language
  • Interactions with advertisements (views, clicks, dismissals)

AdMob shares advertising data with Google's advertising partners to serve relevant ads and measure their performance. This constitutes "sharing" of personal data under some privacy laws (see Sections 7 and 8).

You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalised advertising at any time in your Android device settings under Google > Ads.

AdMob is operated by Google LLC under their Privacy Policy.

1.3 Camera and photos

The Camera Unlock feature (available as an optional paid upgrade) allows you to import a photo from your device's camera or gallery and convert it into a personalised paint-by-number canvas.

Photos are processed entirely on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers, never transmitted to any third party, and never stored outside your device. The camera permission is requested only when you choose to use this feature and is not used for any other purpose.

1.4 In-app purchases

If you make a purchase (Remove Ads or Camera Unlock), the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment card details, billing address, or any payment information. Purchase records are maintained by Google under their Payments Privacy Notice.

1.5 Information we do NOT collect

  • Name, email, phone number, or any account information
  • Precise GPS location
  • Contacts, calendar, or other app data
  • Photos (camera images never leave your device)
  • Health or financial data
  • Background location or microphone access

2. How We Use the Information

  • Analytics data is used exclusively to improve the app: identifying levels with high abandon rates, measuring engagement and session quality, prioritising content updates, and diagnosing performance issues.
  • Advertising data is used by Google AdMob to serve ads and measure their effectiveness. We do not use advertising data for any other purpose.
  • Camera data is used only to generate your personal paint-by-number canvas and is never used for any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following third-party services, which are necessary for the app to function:

4. Data Retention

Firebase Analytics events (GA4)

Gameplay events — such as which levels you play, how long you play them, ratings you give, time spent on the level selection screen, and font settings — are sent to Firebase Analytics as anonymous events. We retain these events for 14 months, after which Google deletes them automatically from their servers. We do not retain independent copies of this data.

Firebase Firestore user documents

A summary of your gameplay progress (levels completed, best times, ratings per level, session history, and streak data) is stored in a Firestore database under your anonymous user ID. This data is retained indefinitely — it does not expire automatically. It is deleted only when:

  • You request deletion by emailing edenapp.contacto@gmail.com (we will process the request within 30 days); or
  • We discontinue the app and delete the database.

This data is linked only to your anonymous ID and cannot be used to identify you.

Advertising data (AdMob)

Google AdMob retains advertising signals, including your Advertising ID and ad interaction data, for approximately 9 months for measurement and fraud prevention purposes, after which it is aggregated or deleted. This is controlled by Google and is subject to their data retention policies.

Local device data

Your level progress, gallery of completed paintings, streak counter, and settings are stored locally on your device only. This data is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app. It is not backed up to any server.

5. Security

All data transmitted to Firebase and AdMob is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Local save data is stored in your device's protected application storage and is not accessible to other apps. No payment data passes through our systems.

6. International Data Transfers

Google Firebase and Google AdMob servers are operated globally, including in the United States. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, your analytics and advertising data will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

Google LLC participates in and has certified its compliance with the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework, providing an appropriate safeguard for these transfers under GDPR Article 46. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
